---
Keeping two documents scrolled to the same spot usually means switching back and forth. This extension links your browser tabs so when you scroll in one, all connected tabs follow to the matching position instantly.
---

How It Works

1. Click the extension icon to open the tab selector
2. Search and select the tabs you want to sync
3. Click "Start Sync" — done

When multiple tabs share the same URL, a smart notification offers to sync them with a single click.

---

Key Features
• Real-time scroll synchronization across any number of tabs
• Command palette with search — find tabs by title or URL, filter by domain
• Manual position adjustment — hold Option (Mac) or Alt (Windows) to fine-tune individual tab alignment
• Smart auto-sync suggestions when duplicate URLs are detected
• URL navigation sync — click a link in one tab and all linked tabs follow
• Domain exclusion — permanently block suggestions for specific sites
• Auto-reconnection after sleep or interruption, no manual action needed
• Full keyboard shortcuts for every action

---

Pages Where Sync Is Not Available
Due to browser security restrictions, scroll sync does not work on certain pages:
• Google services (Docs, Drive, Gmail, Sheets, Slides)
• Web apps such as Figma, JIRA, Notion, and Microsoft Office Online
• Browser internal pages (chrome://, edge://, about:)
• Extension stores and search engine result pages
• PDF files and viewers
• Login and authentication pages
• Special URLs (file://, view-source:, data:)

Restricted tabs appear disabled in the selection list with a tooltip explaining the limitation.
